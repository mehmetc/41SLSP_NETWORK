Welcome...

The library research gateway for the ZHB’s public users and users of regional and special libraries of Central Switzerland (powered by ZHB Lucerne).

Here you find the print holdings of the regional library network and information pointing to academic electronic resources restricted to students and staffs of the University of Lucerne, the Universities of Teacher Education (PH) of Lucerne, Zug and Schwyz and the Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Arts.

When logged in, you benefit from nationwide services like:

  • access to more than 500 academic libraries in Switzerland
  • book orders from other libraries, directly or via interurban courier
  • administration of your account, loans and loan requests

How can I register?

To gain access to all our library services, you need a user account. You can find the relevant instructions here.

To change your login data and personal information, please log in to your SWITCH edu-ID account.

Where am I conducting my search?

Search profiles swisscovery RZS

The multi-level library system swisscovery allows you to conduct searches in both local and Swiss-wide holdings. Swisscovery RZS is the library research gateway of our regional library network in Central Switzerland.

swisscovery RZS - Uni/PH, swisscovery RZS - HSLU and swisscovery RZS - XUND are institutional views offering students and staff tailor-made media content.

The view swisscovery RZS - RegionZentralSchweiz has been designed for our public users and the users of regional and special libraries of Central Switzerland.

The print holdings of the regional network can be found in all four views.

If you want to exclude academic journal articles and chapters of scholarly ebooks, please select "without CDI" in your search profile.

Access to e-media

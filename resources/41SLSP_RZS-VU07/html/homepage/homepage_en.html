Welcome...

The library research gateway for students and staff of the University of Lucerne and the Universities of Teacher Education of Lucerne, Zug and Schwyz.

Here you find:

  • subscription-based e-media content for students and staff of the University of Lucerne and the Universities of Teacher Education of Lucerne, Zug and Schwyz
  • all physical media of the libraries of the Region of Central Switzerland (RZS)
  • all unrestricted and open access e-media content of the libraries of the Region of Central Switzerland (RZS)

When logged in, you benefit from nationwide services like:

  • access to more than 490 academic libraries in Switzerland
  • book orders from other libraries, directly or via interurban courier
  • administration of your account, loans and loan requests

How can I register?

To gain access to all our library services, you need a user account. You can find the relevant instructions here.

To change your login data and personal information, please log in to your SWITCH edu-ID account.

Where am I conducting my search?

Search profiles swisscovery RZS

The multi-level library system swisscovery allows you to conduct searches in both local and Swiss-wide holdings. Swisscovery RZS is the library research gateway of our regional library network in Central Switzerland.

swisscovery RZS - ZHB/Uni/PH and swisscovery RZS - HSLU are institutional views offering students and staff tailor-made media content.

The view swisscovery RZS - RegionZentralSchweiz has been designed for our public users and users of regional and special libraries of Central Switzerland.

The print holdings of the regional network can be found in all three views.

Access to e-media

On-campus at the University of Lucerne, at the Universities of Teacher Education of Lucerne, Zug and Schwyz, and at the ZHB Lucerne, access to subscription-based e-media content is provided via WLAN.

Students and staff of the University of Lucerne and of the Universities of Teacher Education of Lucerne, Zug and Schwyz are advised to use VPN for off-campus access to subscription-based e-media.

Welcome...

The library research gateway for students and staff of the Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Arts (HSLU).

Here you find:

  • subscription-based e-media content for HSLU students and staff
  • all physical media of the libraries of the Region of Central Switzerland (RZS)
  • all unrestricted and open access e-media content of the libraries of the Region of Central Switzerland (RZS)

Access to e-media

On-campus at the HSLU, access to subscription-based e-media content is provided via WLAN.

For off-campus access, HSLU students and staff are advised to use VPN.

Where am I conducting my search?

Search profiles swisscovery RZS

The multi-level library system swisscovery allows you to conduct searches in both local and Swiss-wide holdings. Swisscovery RZS is the library research gateway of our regional library network in Central Switzerland.

swisscovery RZS - Uni/PH and swisscovery RZS - HSLU are institutional views offering students and staff tailor-made media content.

The view swisscovery RZS - RegionZentralSchweiz has been designed for our public users and users of regional and special libraries of Central Switzerland.

The print holdings of the regional network can be found in all three views.
